    What this means here

    Sun positions use the NOAA solar equations (≈0.01° accuracy, 1900–2100). Radiation figures are idealized clear-sky estimates (Kasten–Young air mass, Meinel attenuation) — clouds, haze and altitude will change real values. Times are local to the UTC offset shown in the controls; adjust it if you change locations across time zones.
